How do I know my donation has been sent?

You will get a confirmation email when we have successfully checked your donation has been sent to us.

How do I know my donation has been used?

After we receive a fair amount of donation, we buy books that are more needed. Confirmation of your donation
being used will be sent through your email to keep you a track of how we are using your thankful donation.

Where does my donated books get sent to?

Our aim is to provided places around sub-Saharan Africa where it's the place with most lacking in education around the world.

What kind of books are appropriate to donate?

Any kind of educational purposed books of all age is available for donations Such as math, english, science, art and many other kind of books are welcomed.
